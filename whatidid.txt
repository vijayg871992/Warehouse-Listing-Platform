LEARNING NOTES FOR BEGINNERS
================================================================================

What is a Listing Platform?
---------------------------
Think of it like Craigslist or Facebook Marketplace, but specifically for 
warehouses. People who own warehouses can:
- Post their warehouse like selling something online
- Add photos and descriptions
- Set prices and availability
- Receive inquiries from interested businesses

What is an Analytics System?
----------------------------
Analytics is like keeping score in a game. The platform tracks:
- How many people look at each warehouse (like YouTube view counts)
- Which warehouses are most popular
- Where visitors come from
- What features people search for most
This helps owners understand what customers want!

What is an Approval Workflow?
-----------------------------
It's like submitting homework to a teacher:
1. You submit your work (warehouse listing)
2. Teacher reviews it (admin checks quality)
3. Teacher either approves with a gold star or asks for corrections
4. You get notified of the result
This ensures only good quality warehouses are shown to customers.

What is JWT with Refresh Tokens?
--------------------------------
Imagine you go to an amusement park:
- You get a wristband (JWT token) that lets you on rides
- The wristband expires after a few hours (for security)
- You have a special ticket (refresh token) to get a new wristband
- This way, if someone steals your wristband, it won't work for long!

What is Winston Logger?
----------------------
Winston is like a security camera system that records everything:
- Who logged in and when
- What actions were taken
- If any errors occurred
- Performance information
This helps developers fix problems and improve the system.
